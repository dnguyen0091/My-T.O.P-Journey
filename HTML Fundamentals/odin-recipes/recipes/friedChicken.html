Fried Chicken

A crispy batch of fried chicken is a staple in a southern household. The crisp and crunchy outside paired with the juicy white meat in chicken wings provides a flavourful experience.

Ingredients

  • 4lbs of chicken
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 2 qts of vegetable oil
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut up chicken pieces

  2. In another bowl. Season flour with paprika,salt, and pepper

  3. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk then, a few at a time, put them in the bag with the flour, seal the bag and shake to coat well.

  4. Place the coated chicken on a cookie sheet or tray, and cover with a clean dish towel or waxed paper. LET SIT UNTIL THE FLOUR IS OF A PASTE-LIKE CONSISTENCY. THIS IS CRUCIAL!

  5. Put in as many chicken pieces as the skillet can hold. Brown the chicken in HOT oil on both sides.

  6. When browned, reduce heat and cover skillet; let cook for 30 minutes (the chicken will be cooked through but not crispy). Remove cover, raise heat again, and continue to fry until crispy.

  7. Drain the fried chicken on paper towels. Depending on how much chicken you have, you may have to fry in a few shifts. Keep the finished chicken in a slightly warm oven while preparing the rest.
